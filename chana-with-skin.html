Chana - With Skin

Product Details

Our Chana (With Skin) is sourced from the finest farms, ensuring top quality and nutritional value. These chickpeas, also known as kala chana, are rich in protein, fiber, and essential minerals. The skin provides additional fiber, contributing to better digestion and overall health. Upgrain's Chana (With Skin) is unpolished and minimally processed to retain its natural goodness.
