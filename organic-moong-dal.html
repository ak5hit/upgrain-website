Organic Moong Dal (Split Mung Beans)

Product Details

Experience the light, nutritious, and easy-to-digest qualities of Upgrain's Organic Moong Dal. Sourced from certified organic farms, our moong dal (split mung beans without skin) is a powerhouse of protein and essential nutrients.

It cooks quickly and is incredibly versatile, making it perfect for traditional Indian dals, khichdi, soups, and even desserts. Its delicate flavor makes it a favorite for all age groups. Upgrain ensures that our moong dal is pure, unadulterated, and retains its natural goodness.
